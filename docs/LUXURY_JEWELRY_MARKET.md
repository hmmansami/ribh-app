### The Gap (RIBH Opportunity) üéØ

**Current solutions:**
- Flat monthly fee ($30-50/month typical)
- No risk for store = no skin in the game
- Generic templates, not jewelry-specific
- Focus on email, WhatsApp as afterthought

**RIBH differentiators:**
1. **Pay-per-performance** - Store only pays when sales recovered
2. **30% of recovered revenue** - Aligned incentives
3. **WhatsApp-first** - 2-4x better recovery than email
4. **Jewelry-specific** - Language, timing, psychology tuned for luxury
5. **Arabic-native** - Perfect for Saudi market
6. **Zero upfront cost** - No risk to try

**Price comparison for typical store:**
- Karzoun: $445/year = $37/month (fixed)
- RIBH: $2,160-8,640/month (but ONLY on recovered sales)

**Stores win when:**
- Recovery is successful ‚Üí they're happy to share 30%
- Recovery fails ‚Üí they pay nothing (unlike competitors)
