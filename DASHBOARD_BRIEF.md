# Dashboard Design Brief

## Steve Jobs Principles
1. **Simplicity** - Remove everything unnecessary
2. **Focus** - One metric that matters: MONEY RECOVERED
3. **Say no** - No feature bloat, no clutter
4. **How it works > looks** - Every pixel serves a purpose
5. **Customer experience first** - Merchant sees money, feels dopamine

## The ONE Thing Dashboard Must Do
**Show the merchant they're making money while they sleep**
